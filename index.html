Our Mission

We strive to be your friendly neighborhood spot, not just for eating but for gathering, sharing, laughing, enjoying, taking a break.

Our space welcomes everyone!

Aside from sandwiches, Papa Duke's strives to provide employment and livable wages to local workers, keeping talent in the area and supporting our local community. We also donate bread and stuff.

Our Story

Whether you're stopping in for lunch to take a relaxing break from your hectic schedule, or you're looking to cater your next gathering, the Papa Duke's Team is here for you!

Who is Papa Duke?

Papa Duke's Deli is named after Chip's legendary father, Duke. Learn more about Papa Duke and his legacy in the deli where we have a mural dedicated to his story.
